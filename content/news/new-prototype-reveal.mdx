## Key Innovations

### Magnetic Levitation System

Our custom-designed maglev system achieves a 15mm levitation gap, providing:
- Frictionless movement
- Precise control at high speeds
- Energy regeneration during braking

### Aerodynamic Shell

The carbon fiber composite shell features:
- Wind-tunnel optimized design
- 40% drag reduction vs. previous version
- Modular construction for easy maintenance
