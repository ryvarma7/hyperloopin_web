# Hyperloopin Achieves Top 10 Finish at SpaceX Competition

We are thrilled to announce that Team Hyperloopin has secured a **top 10 finish** at the prestigious SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition 2024!

## The Journey

Our journey to this achievement began over 18 months ago when our team of 60+ dedicated engineers, designers, and strategists came together with a single vision: to push the boundaries of high-speed transportation technology.

### Key Highlights

- **450 km/h top speed** achieved during testing
- **Advanced magnetic levitation system** successfully demonstrated
- **Custom-designed aerodynamic shell** reduced drag by 40%
- **Real-time telemetry system** provided crucial performance data

## What This Means

This recognition from SpaceX validates our innovative approach and the countless hours of hard work put in by every team member. We competed against over 1,000 university teams from around the world, and reaching the top 10 is a testament to our engineering excellence.
